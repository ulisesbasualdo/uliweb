Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

Thank you for using Pausy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Pausy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data generated while using the app remains on your device and is stored locally.

2. Camera & Microphone Access

Pausy requires access to your device's camera and microphone to provide its core functionality. These permissions are used solely within the app on your device. No audio or video data is ever uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any external server or third party. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

Any data created during your use of Pausy is saved exclusively on your device's local storage. We do not have access to this data, and it is never sent to any remote server.

4. Third-Party Analytics & Advertising

Pausy does not use any third-party analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks. We do not display ads of any kind within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Pausy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since the app does not collect any data at all, no information from minors is gathered, stored, or processed.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
